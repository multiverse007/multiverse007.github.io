ASTROVERSE
This is a non-profit organization where the people who are interested in astronomy and also quantum physics are joined together. Here everybody follow their passion in astronomy. On the way they meet so many other enthusiasts who can help them in their studies.

Most of the astronomy content is shared here so nobody has to search up for everything and can save their time. Everyone is encouraged to go deep into the subject and have their fun.
